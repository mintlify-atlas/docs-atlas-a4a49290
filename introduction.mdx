## Mission Overview

Welcome aboard, Cadet! You've arrived at the **Intergalactic Reforestation Unit**, stationed on the edge of the exosphere. Your mission: help planets in crisis - overheating, choking on carbon, and running out of time.

Reforestation Saga is an innovative iOS game that combines environmental storytelling with cutting-edge eye blink detection technology powered by Apple's Vision framework. Control gameplay entirely through your eye blinks as you plant trees across dying planets.

<Note>
  This game requires camera permissions to detect eye blinks. Your camera feed is processed locally on your device and never transmitted or stored.
</Note>

## System Requirements

- iOS device with TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later recommended)
- iOS 14.0 or later
- Xcode 13.0 or later for development
- Camera permissions enabled

<Warning>
  This game uses real-time face tracking and eye detection. Ensure you're in a well-lit environment and position your face clearly within the camera frame for optimal performance.
</Warning>

## About the Technology

Reforestation Saga leverages Apple's Vision framework to detect facial landmarks and calculate Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) in real-time. When both eyes close below a threshold of 0.1, the game registers a blink and shoots a needle to plant trees on the rotating planet.

The game combines:
- **AVFoundation** for camera capture
- **Vision framework** for face landmark detection
- **SpriteKit** for 2D game rendering
- **SwiftUI** for the user interface

<Info>
  Eye blink detection processes every 3rd frame to optimize performance while maintaining responsive controls.
</Info>
