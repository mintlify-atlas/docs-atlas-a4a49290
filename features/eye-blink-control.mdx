Reforestation Saga uses advanced eye blink detection to create an intuitive, hands-free gaming experience. Every blink you make plants a tree in the game world.

## How It Works

The game uses your device's camera to track your eyes in real-time. When you blink, a tree is instantly planted on the rotating planet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Camera Access">
    When you start playing, the game requests access to your device's camera. This is required for eye tracking to work.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Face Detection">
    The game automatically detects your face and tracks both eyes simultaneously using Vision framework technology.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Blink Recognition">
    When both eyes close and reopen, the game registers a blink and triggers the tree planting action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Visual Feedback

<Info>
Your eyes are outlined in gold while the camera is tracking them, so you always know the system is working.
</Info>

### Blink Counter

A blink counter appears in the top-left corner of your screen showing your total blinks during the session:

```swift
blinkLabel.text = "Blink: \(self.blinkCount)"
```

### Tree Indicator

A floating tree indicator above the planet shows where your next tree will be planted. This helps you time your blinks perfectly.

## Camera Controls

### Switching Cameras

You can switch between front and back cameras using the camera rotation button in the bottom-right corner. This flexibility lets you find the best angle for accurate detection.

<Tip>
The front camera usually provides the most reliable eye tracking since it faces toward you.
</Tip>

## Detection Sensitivity

The game uses Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) calculations to determine when your eyes are closed:

```swift
if leftEAR < 0.1 && rightEAR < 0.1 {
    if !self.isEyeClosed {
        self.isEyeClosed = true
    }
}
```

Both eyes must have an aspect ratio below 0.1 for a blink to register. This threshold ensures natural blinks are detected while preventing false triggers.

## Performance Optimization

<Accordion title="Frame Processing">
The game processes every 3rd frame instead of every frame to optimize performance while maintaining responsive blink detection:

```swift
frameCount += 1
guard frameCount % 3 == 0 else { return }
```

This approach balances detection accuracy with battery life and device temperature.
</Accordion>

## First Blink Tutorial

<Note>
When you first enter a game level, a blink alert appears on screen. Simply blink once to dismiss it and start planting trees.
</Note>

This initial prompt ensures you're ready and the eye tracking system has locked onto your face before gameplay begins.

## Tips for Best Results

<Steps>
  <Step title="Good Lighting">
    Play in a well-lit environment. The camera needs to clearly see your eyes for accurate tracking.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Hold Steady">
    Keep your device at a comfortable distance where your entire face is visible in the camera view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Natural Blinks">
    Use your normal blinking motion. Exaggerated blinks aren't necessary—the system detects natural eye closures.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Camera Angle">
    Position the camera so it has a clear view of both your eyes without obstructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The game keeps your screen active during gameplay to maintain continuous eye tracking. This prevents the device from sleeping mid-game.
</Warning>

## Accessibility

Eye blink control makes Reforestation Saga accessible to players who prefer or require hands-free gameplay. The system works naturally without requiring any special equipment beyond your device's built-in camera.
