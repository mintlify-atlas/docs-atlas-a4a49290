## Game Objective

Your mission is to plant trees on rotating planets by shooting needles that hit designated tree zones. Each level requires you to plant a specific number of trees while avoiding water zones and existing trees.

<Note>
  Trees needed per level increases progressively: Level 1 requires 3 trees, Level 2 requires 4 trees, and the count continues to grow based on the formula `base (3) + cycle + position in cycle`.
</Note>

## Blink Detection

### How It Works

The game uses Apple's Vision framework to track your facial landmarks and detect eye blinks:

1. The front-facing camera captures your face in real-time
2. Face landmarks are detected, specifically your left and right eyes
3. Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) is calculated for both eyes
4. When both eyes close (EAR < 0.1), the system registers it as "eyes closed"
5. When you open your eyes again, a blink is counted and a needle is shot

<Info>
  The Eye Aspect Ratio is calculated using vertical and horizontal distances between eye landmark points: `(vertical1 + vertical2) / (2.0 * horizontal)`
</Info>

### Calibration Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Position Your Face">
    Align your face with the camera scanner when prompted during the initial "Verify Identity" screen
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ensure Good Lighting">
    Play in a well-lit environment for accurate eye detection. The camera draws golden outlines around your eyes when detected
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice Blinking">
    Perform deliberate, complete blinks. Partial blinks or squinting may not register
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Camera if Needed">
    Use the camera rotation button in the bottom-right corner to switch between front and back cameras
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Shooting Needles

Each time you blink, a needle is shot from the center of the screen toward the rotating planet. Timing is crucial:

- **Hit a tree zone**: Successfully plants a tree and decreases your trees remaining counter
- **Hit water/ocean zone**: Mission failed - you must restart from Level 1
- **Hit an existing tree**: Mission failed - you must restart from Level 1

<Warning>
  Be precise with your timing! Missing the tree zone or hitting water/trees will end your current run.
</Warning>

### Level Progression

The game features progressive difficulty across levels:

#### Levels 1-5 (Easy)
- Rotation duration: 4 seconds per full rotation
- Rotation direction: Counter-clockwise (consistent)
- Trees needed: 3-7 trees

#### Levels 6-10 (Medium)
- Rotation duration: 3 seconds per full rotation
- Rotation direction: Random (left or right)
- Trees needed: 8-12 trees

#### Levels 11+ (Hard)
- Rotation duration: 1.5-4 seconds (randomized)
- Rotation direction: Random (left or right)
- Trees needed: 13+ trees (increases with level)

<Info>
  Your highest level achieved is saved automatically using UserDefaults and displayed on the home screen.
</Info>

### User Interface

**Top Left - Progress Bar**
- Shows trees remaining vs total pollution (trees needed)
- Updates in real-time as you plant trees

**Top Right - Level & Sound**
- Current level number
- Sound toggle button to mute/unmute background music

**Bottom Right - Camera Switch**
- Button to rotate between front and back cameras

**Blink Counter**
- Displays total blinks detected (top left of camera view)
- Helps you verify the eye detection is working

## Game States

### Initial Blink Alert

When you start playing, a "Blink Alert" appears prompting you to blink. Your first blink dismisses this alert and plants the first tree.

### Level Complete

When you successfully plant all required trees for a level:
- A "Level Complete" popup appears
- The game automatically advances to the next level after 1.2 seconds
- Your highest level is updated if you surpassed your previous record

### Mission Failed

If you hit a water zone or existing tree:
- A "Mission Failed" popup appears showing your final level
- Displays whether you achieved a new high score
- Provides a "Next Mission" button to restart from Level 1

<Note>
  Different sound effects play based on failure reason: "water" sound for ocean hits, "failed" sound for hitting existing trees.
</Note>

## Audio Experience

- **Home Screen**: "IntroSpace" background music sets the space exploration mood
- **Gameplay**: "GameEasy" background music during active play
- **Success**: Sound effect when completing a level
- **Failure**: Context-specific sound effects (water splash or general failure)

Use the sound toggle button to mute all audio while keeping the game fully playable.

## Tips for Success

1. **Time your blinks**: Watch the planet rotation and blink when the tree zone is approaching the center
2. **Practice deliberate blinks**: Quick, complete blinks register more reliably than slow closures
3. **Stay centered**: Keep your face aligned with the camera for consistent detection
4. **Monitor the blink counter**: If it's not increasing when you blink, adjust your position or lighting
5. **Learn the patterns**: Higher levels have randomized rotation, so develop timing intuition

<Warning>
  The game disables idle timer during gameplay to prevent screen lock. Remember to manually lock your device when finished playing to save battery.
</Warning>
